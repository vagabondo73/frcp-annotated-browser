Rule 2. One Form of Action

Layer: FRCP · Category: Scope/Purpose
There is one form of action—the civil action.
Notes: Notes of Advisory Committee—1937: This rule modifies 28 U.S.C. [former] § 384 (Suits in equity, when not sustainable). It unifies the formerly separate proceedings at law and in equity into a single civil action, following the model of code states such as New York (1848).
