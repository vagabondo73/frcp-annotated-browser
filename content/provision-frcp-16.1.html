Rule 16.1. Multidistrict Litigation

Layer: FRCP · Category: Pleading
(a) Applicability. This rule applies to actions transferred to a district court under 28 U.S.C. §1407 for coordinated or consolidated pretrial proceedings. (b) Scheduling Orders in Multidistrict Litigation. The court's obligation to issue a scheduling order under Rule 16(b) applies to cases transferred under 28 U.S.C. §1407, but the content requirements of Rule 16(b)(3)(A) do not apply. (c) Interlocutory Orders in Multidistrict Litigation. An order entered in an action transferred under 28 U.S.C. §1407 before remand governs the action after remand to the extent practicable.
Notes: Added effective December 1, 2024. Addresses management of multidistrict litigation (MDL) proceedings under 28 U.S.C. § 1407.
