Rule 3. Commencing an Action

Layer: FRCP · Category: Commencement
A civil action is commenced by filing a complaint with the court.
Notes: Notes of Advisory Committee—1937: Rule 5(e) defines what constitutes filing with the court. This rule provides that the first step in an action is the filing of the complaint. Under Rule 4(a) this is to be followed forthwith by issuance of a summons. Whether filing alone tolls a state statute of limitations depends on whether state or federal law applies (Walker v. Armco Steel Corp., 1980).
