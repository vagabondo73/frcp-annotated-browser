Rule 21. Misjoinder and Nonjoinder of Parties

Layer: FRCP · Category: Parties
Misjoinder of parties is not a ground for dismissing an action. On motion or on its own, the court may at any time, on just terms, add or drop a party. The court may also sever any claim against a party.
Notes: Notes of Advisory Committee—1937: Severance under Rule 21 results in two separate actions, which may affect finality for purposes of appeal and preclusion, unlike separate trials under Rule 42(b).
